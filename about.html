About Our Firm

Excellence in Legal Services Since 2012

Our Legacy

Established in 2012, under the leadership of Adv. Meenakshi Yadav, Meenakshi Yadav & Associates has emerged as a premier law firm in the NCR region. Renowned for our dedication to client success and legal excellence, we have earned acclaim across diverse legal fields.

We combine decade of experience with innovative legal solutions to address the most challenging legal issues faced by our clients. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure the best possible outcomes for every case we handle.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations through expert legal counsel, proactive prevention of workplace harassment, and fostering equitable environments by integrating legal compliance with social justice and education.
