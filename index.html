Who we are

Meenakshi Yadav & Associates is a distinguished law firm established in 2020, dedicated to delivering comprehensive legal solutions across various domains. With over 12 years of collective experience, our team specializes in matrimonial, civil, criminal, employment (labor law), taxation, and the Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) laws.

At the helm is Advocate Meenakshi Yadav, a seasoned legal professional with a Master's in Corporate Law and International Human Rights. Her leadership and vision have been instrumental in guiding the firm to address complex legal challenges effectively.

Complementing our legal services, the Yashashish Foundation operates under the guidance of Advocate Meenakshi Yadav and Advocate Ashish Yadav. This non-governmental organization is committed to providing pro bono legal services and community support across the NCR region.

Together, Meenakshi Yadav & Associates and the Yashashish Foundation embody a commitment to legal excellence and social responsibility, working tirelessly to uphold justice and empower communities.
