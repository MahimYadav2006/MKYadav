Who we are

Meenakshi Yadav & Associates is a distinguished law firm established in 2020, dedicated to delivering comprehensive legal solutions across various domains. With over 12 years of collective experience, our team specializes in matrimonial, civil, criminal, employment (labor law), taxation, and the Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) laws. Our commitment lies in providing client-centric services that are both legally sound and pragmatically viable, ensuring seamless, efficient, and integrated legal assistance.

At the helm is Advocate Meenakshi Yadav, a seasoned legal professional with a Master's in Corporate Law and International Human Rights. Her leadership and vision have been instrumental in guiding the firm to address complex legal challenges effectively. Beyond her legal practice, she is deeply invested in social justice and legal education, regularly conducting workshops and awareness sessions to foster a more informed society.

Complementing our legal services, the Yashashish Foundation operates under the guidance of Advocate Meenakshi Yadav and Advocate Ashish Yadav. This non-governmental organization is committed to providing pro bono legal services and community support across the NCR region. Our mission is to ensure access to justice for all, with a particular focus on underprivileged communities. Through legal awareness workshops, free consultations, and educational initiatives, we strive to create a more equitable legal system. The foundation also offers scholarships to aspiring law students and supports various social justice causes.

Together, Meenakshi Yadav & Associates and the Yashashish Foundation embody a commitment to legal excellence and social responsibility, working tirelessly to uphold justice and empower communities.
