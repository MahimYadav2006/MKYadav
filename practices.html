POSH Compliance

We provide comprehensive services under the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition & Redressal) Act, 2013. Our expertise includes:

  • Formation and training of Internal Complaints Committees (ICC)
  • Policy drafting and implementation
  • Conducting workplace awareness programs
  • Managing internal inquiries with legal precision
  • Representing clients in POSH-related court appeals
  • Regular compliance audits and updates

With Adv. Meenakshi Yadav's experience as a Member of the Local Complaints Committee in Gurugram, we deliver authoritative guidance in creating safer workplaces.
