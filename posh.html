Advanced Training & Awareness Programs

Our segmented training approach ensures every member of your organization understands their rights, responsibilities, and the mechanisms available for redressal. We create tailored programs that speak to different audiences within your workplace.

Employee Awareness Programs

Comprehensive sessions for both white-collar and blue-collar workforce covering rights, responsibilities, and redressal mechanisms with interactive modules.

Managerial & Leadership Workshops

Focused sessions for managers and top management on complaint handling, compliance strategy, accountability, and creating inclusive work environments.

IC Capacity Building Programs

Specialized training for Internal Committee Members on investigation procedures, inquiry processes, documentation standards, and legal requirements.

Train-the-Trainer Certification

Comprehensive certification program to develop qualified in-house POSH trainers for continuous learning and organizational sustainability.

Professional Complaint Handling

We provide qualified external members and expert support for managing sensitive complaints that require careful handling, ensuring fairness, confidentiality, and adherence to legal procedures throughout the process.

External Member Services

MSY provides qualified External Members to Internal Committees ensuring unbiased, fair inquiries with professional expertise and legal knowledge.

NCW Escalation Support

Expert handling of sensitive complaints that escalate to forums like the National Commission for Women, with strategic legal representation and guidance.

Comprehensive Policy Framework

We develop robust policy frameworks and provide complete documentation support to ensure your organization meets all legal requirements while creating clear, actionable guidelines for all stakeholders.

POSH Policy Development

Internal Committee Constitution

Government Compliance
